Directions

Use your phone's web browser for the best experience.
  1. On iPhone or Android, press "Record" button
  2. Select new recording option ('New Video' on iPhone, or equivelant option on Android)
  3. Hold phone parallel with your face
  4. Press record
  5. To get a more diverse range of lighting, keep your phone's camera on your face with your arms horizontal, then rotate 360 degrees
  6. Stop recording
  7. Recording should be around <= 8 seconds long and upto 2MB in size
  8. Please make sure yours is the only face that appears throughout the video
  9. Press 'Upload' button
  10. Please wait for the browser to redirect you after pressing 'Upload' button
